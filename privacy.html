Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At VitalAI, we take your health privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle your biometric data and our commitment to security.

1. Data Collection

We do not store your personal biometric data on our servers. VitalAI is designed as a "Local-First" application, meaning all analysis happens directly on your device.

2. Camera Usage

The application requires camera access to perform photoplethysmogram (PPG) analysis. No video footage is ever recorded, uploaded, or shared. The stream is processed in real-time in your browser memory and discarded immediately.

3. Local Storage

Your scan history is saved to your browser's localStorage. This data remains on your physical device and is not accessible by VitalAI staff or third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can clear your entire scan history at any time via the Settings page. Clearing browser cookies/cache will also remove your local health profile.
